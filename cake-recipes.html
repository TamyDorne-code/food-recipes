Classic Vanilla Cake
Ingredients: Flour, sugar, eggs, butter, milk, baking powder, vanilla extract
Instructions: Preheat oven to 180°C. Cream butter and sugar, add eggs and vanilla. Mix in flour and baking powder, then milk. Pour into pan and bake for 30-35 minutes.

Chocolate Fudge Cake
Ingredients: Flour, cocoa powder, sugar, eggs, butter, baking soda, milk, chocolate
Instructions: Mix dry ingredients. Cream butter and sugar, add eggs. Combine with dry mix and milk. Stir in melted chocolate. Bake at 175°C for 35 minutes.

Carrot Cake
Ingredients: Flour, sugar, eggs, oil, grated carrots, baking powder, cinnamon, walnuts
Instructions: Mix flour, baking powder, cinnamon. Beat eggs and sugar, add oil and carrots. Combine with dry mix, add walnuts. Bake at 180°C for 40 minutes.

Red Velvet Cake
Ingredients: Flour, cocoa powder, sugar, eggs, butter, buttermilk, red food coloring, baking soda, vinegar
Instructions: Cream butter and sugar, add eggs. Mix in dry ingredients, buttermilk, and coloring. Add baking soda and vinegar. Bake at 175°C for 30 minutes.

Fruit Cake
Ingredients: Mixed dried fruits, nuts, flour, sugar, eggs, butter, baking powder, spices
Instructions: Mix all ingredients and pour into a greased cake tin. Bake at 160°C for 1 hour.

Lemon Drizzle Cake
Ingredients: Flour, sugar, eggs, butter, lemon zest, lemon juice, baking powder
Instructions: Cream butter and sugar, add eggs and lemon zest. Mix in flour and baking powder. Bake at 180°C for 30 minutes. Drizzle with lemon juice and sugar.
