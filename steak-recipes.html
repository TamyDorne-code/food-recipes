Classic Steak
Ingredients: Steak, salt, pepper, butter, garlic, rosemary

Beef Stew
Ingredients: Beef chunks, potatoes, carrots, onion, beef broth, tomato paste, herbs
Instructions: Brown beef, add vegetables, broth, and tomato paste. Simmer for 2 hours until tender.
Hearty beef stew
Loaded with veggies
Rich and comforting
Serve with bread
Pork Chops
Ingredients: Pork chops, salt, pepper, garlic powder, olive oil, rosemary
Instructions: Season pork chops, sear in olive oil, add rosemary. Cook until golden and juices run clear.
Juicy pork chops
Serve with rosemary

Roast Lamb
Ingredients: Lamb leg, garlic, rosemary, olive oil, salt, pepper
Instructions: Rub lamb with garlic, rosemary, oil, salt, and pepper. Roast at 180°C for 1.5 hours. Rest before slicing.
Tender roast lamb
Serve with herbs
Perfect for holidays
Add roasted veggies
