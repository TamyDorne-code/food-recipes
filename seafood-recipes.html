Grilled Salmon
Ingredients: Salmon fillets, olive oil, lemon, salt, pepper, dill
Instructions: Brush salmon with olive oil, season with salt, pepper, and dill. Grill for 5-7 minutes per side. Squeeze lemon before serving.

Garlic Shrimp
Ingredients: Shrimp, garlic, butter, parsley, lemon, salt

Fish Tacos
Ingredients: White fish fillets, taco shells, cabbage, lime, cilantro, sour cream, chili powder, salt
Instructions: Season fish with chili powder and salt, grill or pan-fry until cooked. Fill taco shells with fish, cabbage, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime. Top with sour cream.

Seafood Paella
Ingredients: Rice, shrimp, mussels, squid, bell pepper, tomato, onion, garlic, saffron, olive oil, chicken broth
Instructions: Sauté onion, garlic, and bell pepper in olive oil. Add rice and saffron, stir. Add broth and tomatoes, simmer. Add seafood and cook until rice is tender and seafood is done.

Steamed Mussels
Ingredients: Mussels, white wine, garlic, parsley, butter, lemon
Instructions: Sauté garlic in butter, add mussels and white wine. Cover and steam until mussels open. Sprinkle with parsley and serve with lemon.
