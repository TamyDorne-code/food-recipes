Matooke
Ingredients: Matooke / plantains, salt, water, tomatoes, onions, green pepper, carrots and parsley.
Instructions: Peel and slice the matooke. Boil with a little water and salt until tender. Cut the rest of the ingredients into small pieces and fry them in a different pot. Once they are soft and fragrant, mix them with the matooke and serve.
Serve when hot.
Serve with avocado and greens.
Greek Salad
Ingredients: Tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, vegan feta, olives, olive oil, oregano
Instructions: Chop vegetables, mix in a bowl. Add vegan feta and olives. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle oregano. Toss and serve.

Chickpea Curry
Ingredients: Chickpeas, coconut milk, onion, garlic, ginger, curry powder, spinach
Instructions: Sauté onion, garlic, and ginger. Add curry powder, chickpeas, and coconut milk. Simmer, add spinach, cook until wilted. Serve with rice.
Creamy chickpea curry

Irish potatoes
Ingredients: Potatoes, oil, tomatoes, onions, parsley, bell peppers, garlic and desired spices.
Instructions: Peel potatoes and cut into halves. In a pan pour a very little amount of oil and add the potatoes. Mix and add the onions, bell peppers, garlic and salt. Stir for a while and add small water. Once the potatoes are cooked, add the grated tomatoes and parsley. Wait for 5-15 minutes and serve.
Serve when hot

Bean Soup
Ingredients: Beans, carrots, celery, onion, garlic, vegetable broth, thyme
Instructions: Sauté onion, garlic, carrots, and celery. Add beans, broth, and thyme. Simmer until beans are tender. Serve hot.
Hearty bean soup
Good for lunch
Sweet potatoes
Ingredients: Sweet potatoes, oil
Instructions: Wash the sweet potatoes and cut them into cubes. Toss with oil and seasonings. In a baking dish, arrange the sweet potatoes in a single layer. Bake at 400°F (200°C) for 25-30 minutes or until tender. Serve warm.
