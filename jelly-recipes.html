Classic Strawberry Jelly
Ingredients: Strawberries, sugar, lemon juice, gelatin, water
Instructions: Blend strawberries, heat with sugar and lemon juice. Dissolve gelatin in water, mix with strawberry blend. Pour into molds and chill until set.

Orange Jelly Cups
Ingredients: Orange juice, sugar, gelatin, orange zest, water
Instructions: Heat orange juice, sugar, and zest. Dissolve gelatin in water, mix with juice. Pour into cups and chill until firm.

Grape Jelly
Ingredients: Grape juice, sugar, lemon juice, gelatin, water
Instructions: Heat grape juice, sugar, and lemon juice. Dissolve gelatin in water, mix with juice. Pour into jars and chill until set.
Sweet grape jelly

Mixed Fruit Jelly
Ingredients: Mixed fruit juice, sugar, gelatin, lemon juice, fruit pieces
Instructions: Heat juice, sugar, and lemon juice. Dissolve gelatin in water, mix with juice. Add fruit pieces, pour into molds, and chill.
