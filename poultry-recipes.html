Roast Chicken
Ingredients: Whole chicken, olive oil, garlic, lemon, rosemary, salt, pepper
Instructions: Rub chicken with oil, garlic, lemon, rosemary, salt, and pepper. Roast at 190°C for 1 hour 20 minutes.

Chicken Curry
Ingredients: Chicken breast, curry powder, coconut milk, onion, garlic, ginger, oil, salt
Instructions: Sauté onion, garlic, and ginger. Add chicken and brown. Add curry powder and coconut milk, simmer until chicken is cooked. Serve with rice.
Creamy chicken curry
Serve with rice
Add fresh cilantro / coriander

Roast Duck
Ingredients: Duck, salt, pepper, orange, thyme, honey
Instructions: Season duck, stuff with orange and thyme, roast at 180°C for 1 hour. Glaze with honey before serving.
Crispy roast duck
Serve with orange
Perfect for celebrations

Roast Goose
Ingredients: Goose, salt, pepper, apple, onion, thyme
Instructions: Season goose, stuff with apple and onion, roast at 180°C for 2 hours. Rest before carving.
